    Who are we?

    For over 25 years, Miltonia has been providing colour marketing tools such as fan decks, color cards, and other similar color sampling tools necessary for inventive advertisement of paint companies' products. The strategy is simple; give customers what they require, in the finest way possible. That's why we emphasize on customising every project individually, depending on the customers’ needs, and then manufacturing a product of the highest quality possible.
